Dear My Sister From Another Mother ❤️

Happy Birthday! 🎂✨

Some people enter our lives as friends, but with time they become something much more special — they become family. You are one of those rare people for me. You are not just my best friend; you are my sister from another mother, my female version, and someone who understands me in ways very few people do.

Sometimes I wonder how two completely different people can be so similar at the same time. The way we think, the way we react, the way we can laugh at the most random things — it feels like I found a person who carries a little piece of myself.

Thank you for being the person I can talk to without pretending, the person who understands my silence, supports me during difficult times, and celebrates my happiness like it is your own. Our conversations, jokes, arguments, and countless memories are things I will always treasure.

On your special day, I wish you a life full of happiness, success, peace, and beautiful surprises. May every dream you have slowly turn into reality, and may you always have the strength to overcome every challenge that comes your way.

And yes... congratulations on becoming one year closer to becoming Pasher Bashar Aunty 😂❤️. But no matter how many birthdays pass, always remember that you will forever be the same crazy, amazing, and wonderful person who makes life a little brighter.

Thank you for being my friend, my sister, and my female version. And taratari tr bhabi khoj 😌🫰 Stay happy, stay blessed, and keep smiling always.

From your brother🩷❤️
